Our Story

Who We Are

The Gathering of Presbyterian Ministers' Children is a vibrant community dedicated to spiritual growth, mutual support, and biblical teaching. We believe in the power of fellowship and the importance of maintaining strong faith foundations.

Our Vision

To create a supportive network where children of Presbyterian ministers can grow spiritually, develop leadership skills, and maintain lifelong connections rooted in Christian values.

Our Values

Faith, Integrity, Community, Compassion, and Continuous Learning. These core values guide all our activities and interactions.

About PMC

A gathering of Presbyterian Ministers' Children, committed to faith, community, and spiritual growth.
